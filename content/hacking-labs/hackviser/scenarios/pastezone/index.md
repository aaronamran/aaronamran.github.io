<p class="lead mb-4">An urgent midnight intelligence alert has triggered a full-scale response: sensitive data belonging to the Ministry of Energy has been discovered for sale on PasteZone, one of the most notorious illegal platforms on the dark web. Cybercriminals use this site to anonymously share details of compromised systems, vulnerabilities, and stolen data. Preliminary investigations have confirmed that the leaked content originates directly from the Ministry of Energy’s systems, posing a significant security threat.
<br /><br />
Your mission is to identify the individuals or groups responsible for this breach, gather intelligence on PasteZone’s administrators, and collect actionable evidence.</p>
